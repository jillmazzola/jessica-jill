About

Jessica

WHO

Jessica Mazzola

WHAT

Reporter / Editor / Writer

WHERE

Chester, NY

WHEN

After starting my career in radio, I made the transition to news reporting in 2009. I have a Bachelor’s Degree in Communications and Political Science from Fordham University, and a Master’s in Continuing Education from Harvard University.

WHY

From my grade school days of interviewing aunts and uncles during family vacations, writing has always been a passion for me. I love to mine everyday life for beautiful, relatable stories.

Jill

WHO

Jill Mazzola

WHAT

Designer / Writer

WHERE

Monroe, NY

WHEN

I graduated from Parsons the New School for Design with a BFA in Fashion Design in 2014. Since then, I’ve worked in marketing, event planning, fashion design, and merchandising.

WHY

No matter what I’m working on -- whether it’s a private label fashion collection or a children’s cartoon -- storytelling is a huge component! While it might not seem like it, writing is a very visual medium, at least to me. The same way I trained to draft technical drawings, I try to paint pictures with words.
